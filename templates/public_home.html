About

Purpose of the bOPV E-Portal

This website supports collection and review of safety data related to bOPV vaccine. It is designed for adverse event collection, AEFI/ADR reporting, RMP monitoring, PSUR/PBRER preparation and signal review.

Minimum Valid Case Criteria

  • Identifiable patient
  • Identifiable reporter
  • Suspected product
  • Suspected adverse event or reaction
